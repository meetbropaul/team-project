Direction
By Car
You can find Amusement-park at the following address:
Amusement-park
2880 Broadway, New York,NY 10025,USA
Amusement-park is located between the M1 and the M6 and is well signposted from both motorways. Depending on your direction, the nearest motorway exits are: M1 Northbound – Junction 23a
M1 Southbound – Junction 28
M6 Northbound – Junction 15
M6 Southbound – Junction 16 

           By Train
           Visitors can travel to Amusement Park by train, via several popular destinations. Visit thetrainline.com  to search for train times and prices.

           Uttoxeter Train Station:
           Uttoxeter station is the nearest train station to Alton Towers, just 10 miles from the resort. Uttoxeter station is just a 20 minute drive away from the resort or 30 minutes by bus.
           
           Stoke on Trent Station:
           Stoke on Trent rail station is 15 miles from Amusement Park. A local taxi would take around 25 minutes to the resort.
