Direction to Adventure-park

By Car

You can find Adventure-park at the following address:
Adventure-park
2880 Broadway, New York,NY 10025,USA
Adventure-park is located between the M1 and the M6 and is well signposted from both motorways. Depending on your direction, the nearest motorway exits are: M1 Northbound – Junction 23a
M1 Southbound – Junction 28
M6 Northbound – Junction 15
M6 Southbound – Junction 16



By Train

Visitors can travel to Adventure Park by train, via several popular destinations. Visit thetrainline.com to search for train times and prices.
Uttoxeter Train Station:
Uttoxeter station is the nearest train station to Fountain Towers, just 10 miles from the resort. Uttoxeter station is just a 20 minute drive away from the resort or 30 minutes by bus.
Stoke on Trent Station:
Stoke on Trent rail station is 15 miles from Adventure Park. A local taxi would take around 25 minutes to the resort.
